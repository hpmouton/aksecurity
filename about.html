ABOUT

AK Security strives to:

- Empower staff members with beneficial skills and knowledge not only beneficial for the organization but for our clients.
- Offer all our clients premium services and a great value for their money.
- Offer our clients a simple but effective solution to best suit their needs.
- Offer custom made solutions, we maintain our clients differently and exclusively.


MISSION

Our mission is to provide protection and security to our clients through a personalized service tailored to their specific needs, ultimately the safety and security of the client’s staff, premises, assets and the general public is our highest priority.


VISION

We are committed to the ongoing improvement of the services we provide to our clients. By investing in and developing our most important assets, our staff, we aim to achieve all our goals and exceed our client’s expectations. Through our commitment to high standards it is our vision to earn the trust of our client’s by delivering the best quality security services within Namibia.

VALUES

These core values provide the inspiration behind our business operations and form the basis for our service. We not only expect our employees to adhere to these standards, we also act according to these core values daily.

  • FOCUS
    AK Security is service-oriented, committed and keeps to its agreements. The Client is our central focus at all times. Surrounding the Client are the Employees and Quality.
  • INTEGRITY
    AK Security is: transparent, sincere and careful. Our clients trust us and assume that we will treat their property, employees and clients with care.
  • RELIABILITY
    Above all, AK Security is reliable, professional, flexible and proactive.
  • SAFETY
    We are committed to the safety of all our employees and clients.
  • TEAMWORK
    We are one company, one team.
